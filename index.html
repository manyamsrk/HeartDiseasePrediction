About Project(Cardiovascular Disease Prediction)

Health care is an in evitable task to be done in human life,especially the silent heart attack and heart disease is one of the most significant causes of mortality in the world today.

Most of the people around the world are experiencing time scheduled and stressful work life, which often leads to increase in the percentage of healthy people affected by heart diseases.

Prediction of cardiovascular disease in the area of clinical data analysis is really challenging. Terabytes of data are produced and stored day-today life because of fast growth in, “Information Technology‟.

So, here we done some work to find prediction of heart attack using Machine Learning algorithms like Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, KNN and in Ensemble approach we use methods like Bagging, Boosting, Voting techniques.
